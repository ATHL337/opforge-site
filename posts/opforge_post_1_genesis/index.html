üîÅ Routing Infrastructure (Phase 1)#

This phase implements multi-hop routing from RED_NET to the internet via chained VyOS routers. It emulates a real-world multi-zone topology with enforced segmentation and control points.

Routing Flow:#

             [opf-rt-inet (eth1:192.168.1.25)] ‚Äî Bridged to internet
  • opf-rt-red is the edge router for RED_NET and the default gateway for red team boxes.
  • opf-rt-inet serves as a central hub with a bridged NIC to the physical network, allowing internet access from controlled segments.
